Use this matrix for session-zero selection and mid-campaign respecialization conversations.

|---|---|---|---|---|---|

| Wild | Skirmish survivor | Medium | Medium | Steady | Ranger, Monk, Druid |
| Veil | Tempo controller | High | Medium | Mid spike | Rogue, Bard, Wizard |
| Forge | Defensive support | Medium | Low | Steady | Cleric, Paladin, Artificer-like |
| Storm | Burst skirmisher | Medium | Medium | Fast early | Monk, Ranger, Sorcerer |

| Hunger | Predatory snowball | High | High | Volatile | Barbarian, Warlock, Ranger |
| Ruin | Siege breaker | Medium | High | Late spike | Fighter, Paladin, Sorcerer |
| Star | Precision support | Medium | Low | Late payoff | Wizard, Cleric, Bard |

## Quick Pick Heuristic
- Want reliable frontline pressure: War, Bone, Chain.
- Want control and tricks: Veil, Mirror, Tide, Dusk.
- Want team support and leadership: Forge, Dawn, Star.
- Want high-risk/high-reward aggression: Hunger, Ruin, Storm.
