# Ascendency Wars Factions (Starter Set)

## The Basalt Crown
Militant dwarven command houses of Kharad-Vul, focused on tunnel denial and survival under siege.

## The Rootbound Choir
Jungle mystics bonded to colossal predatory flora who oppose reckless expansion.

## The Cinder Concord
Relic traders and intelligence brokers who manipulate all sides through debt and secrets.

## The Hollow Synod
Underscar theocrats and flesh-crafters seeking to merge all divine sparks into one host.
