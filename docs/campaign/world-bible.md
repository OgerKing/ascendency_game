# Ascendency Wars World Bible (v1)

## Core Premise
Ascendency Wars is a high-fantasy death world where jungle overgrowth, predatory megafauna, and planar scars are as dangerous as armies. Characters awaken dormant divine cores and are pulled into a global struggle among rising demigods called Ascendants.

## Tone and Genre
- Heroic dark fantasy with survival pressure
- Civilization is concentrated in fortified vertical settlements
- Travel between safe zones is dangerous and expensive
- Divine power is visible, contested, and politically destabilizing

## Planetary Identity: Vharos
- **Surface:** storm-wrapped jungles, fungal canyons, vine-choked ruins
- **Underscar:** fractured Underdark network beneath mountain roots
- **Sky:** red auroras during divine storms called Crownflame Nights
- **Ancient Layer:** collapsed temple-cities from pre-cataclysm empires

## Campaign Launch Region
The campaign starts in **Kharad-Vul**, a dwarven mountain city under coordinated Underdark assault. The surrounding region includes:
- **The Verdant Maw**
- **The Shatterveins**
- **Emberfall Crossing**
